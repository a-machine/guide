Welcome to ADA!

This is a quickstart guide for you to learn about how to utilize the ADA-pipeline to publishing a book.

Logon here to make your book.

The ADA-Pipeline joins together open-source platforms for book publishing.

What you will learn here:

ADA supports realtime collaborative authoring with an online word processor and publishing as multi-format using Git. You can distribute your book as: A website, paged website, PDF, print-on-demand, eBook, and more.

  1. How to prepare your public Git repository for storing your book data.

  2. To setup your book's online collaborative word processor.

  3. Invite your team to collaborate on writing online.

  4. Publishing your book.
